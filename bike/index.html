Our Story

NoHassls.com was borne out of frustrations when searching for insurance quotes. These frustrations included holding on the phone, giving each company the same information and wasting many hours in the process. Comparison sites did not solve the problems - it re-directed to the insurance company’s website and starting the process all over again. The Founders, became passionate about finding a better, faster, and more efficient way of getting an insurance quote without giving away too much personal information.

The Founders, began thinking about re-imaging the way consumers search for insurance and how insurance companies provide quotes and services. How can both benefit from a new way of connecting consumers and insurance companies in a timely, more efficient and hassle-free way. NoHassls is designed and developed by a team with deep experience in financial services markets, technology and with a passion for fintech and disruptive technology. The result is: www.nohassls.com
